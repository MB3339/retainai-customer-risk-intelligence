Executive Summary

This report analyzes customer churn risk and loyalty drivers for a subscription-based company, offering actionable recommendations for retention improvement. The predictive model demonstrates strong performance in identifying at-risk customers for targeted intervention. Key loyalty drivers include longer-term contracts and the use of security and tech support services. Conversely, customers using Fiber Optic internet, those with specific phone service configurations (none or multiple lines), and those opting for streaming services or paperless billing exhibit a higher propensity to churn. The company can leverage these insights for data-driven strategies to proactively engage vulnerable segments and reinforce loyalty.
